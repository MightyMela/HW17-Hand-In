Pawsitive Perspectives

Pawsitive Perspectives is developing a redesigned homepage and more clear navigation for applying for a service dog. We are empowering our clients to understand every step of the process, from application to receiving their dog and beyond

Problem Statement

We believe by improving the homepage design to make it easier for clients to Log In as well as clarifying the Apply For Dog button for people with disabilities will provide them with greater confidence in getting their service dog
